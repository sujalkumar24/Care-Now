# Care-Now
🏥 Care Now - Online Doctor Consultation & Medicine Ordering
Care Now is your one-stop solution for quick and secure healthcare from the comfort of your home. Consult with certified doctors via video call and order prescribed medicines directly through Medihub—our trusted pharmacy partner.

🌐 Features
🩺 Video Consultation: Speak with qualified doctors online through secure video calls.

💊 Medicine Ordering: Get prescribed medicines delivered via Medihub with just a few taps.

📅 Appointment Booking: Schedule consultations at your convenience.

🔒 Secure & Private: End-to-end encrypted calls and secure data handling.

📱 Cross-platform Access: Available via mobile, tablet, and desktop browsers.

🚀 Getting Started
1. Sign Up / Log In
Create an account or sign in with your credentials to access all features.

2. Book a Consultation
Choose your health concern or specialist.

Pick a date and time.

Join the video call when it's time.

3. Get a Prescription
After your consultation, the doctor may issue a digital prescription.

4. Order Medicines via Medihub
View your prescription.

Select "Order from Medihub".

Add to cart, confirm delivery details, and pay online or on delivery.
